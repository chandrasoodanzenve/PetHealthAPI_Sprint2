# Value Realization & Review Framework

## 1. Value Realization Measurement (ROI)

We track the success of our product intelligence platform through three lenses:

- **Financial Impact**: 15% expected lift in Lifetime Value (LTV) due to prescriptive upsells.
- **Operational Efficiency**: 40% reduction in manual customer support audits via proactive health alerts.
- **System Stability**: 99.9% uptime target through automated DR failover validation.

## 2. Quarterly Intelligence Review (QIR) Mechanism

To ensure consistency, the Analytics CoE will hold a review every 90 days.

- **Participants**: Lead Architect, Product Owner, and Data Engineering Lead.
- **Agenda**:
  1. **Model Audit**: Do Health Scores still accurately predict user churn?
  2. **Logic Recalibration**: Update RICE-R prioritization weights based on current business goals.
  3. **Drift Detection**: Review infrastructure sync status from Terraform reports.
