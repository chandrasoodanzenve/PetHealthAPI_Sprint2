#  Lifecycle Trajectory & Optimization Model

## 1. Trajectory Scoring Methodology

A user's trajectory is calculated by comparing the current month's health score vs. the previous month.

- **Upward (Growth)**: Current Score > Previous Score (+10%).
- **Stagnant (Plateau)**: Score difference < 5%.
- **Downward (Decline)**: Current Score < Previous Score (-15%).

## 2. Cohort Optimization Recommendations (Strategies)

- **Accelerate Seed to Explorer**: Trigger "Add your first vaccination" notification.
- **Prevent Champion Decline**: Offer "Premium Milestone Rewards" for users with 6 months of stability.
- **Reactivate Sleepers**: Send personalized "Pet Health Recap" emails to show lost value.
