#  Prescriptive Analytics & Decision Framework

## 1. Decision Categories

We automate decisions across four strategic functions:

- **Product**: UI/UX interventions and onboarding.
- **Growth**: Upsell offers and referral triggers.
- **Retention**: Churn prevention and loyalty rewards.
- **Customer Success**: Proactive health support.

## 2. Analytics-to-Action Rules

- **Insight**: Latency > 1000ms ➔ **Action**: Log Anomaly + Notify DevOps.
- **Insight**: High Health + Free Plan ➔ **Action**: Upsell to "PetHealth Pro".
- **Insight**: 0 Activity in 14 days ➔ **Action**: Trigger "We miss you" Email.
