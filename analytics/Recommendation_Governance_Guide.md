#  Automated Recommendation Governance Guide

## 1. Ethical Guidelines
- **Frequency Cap**: No more than 2 automated recommendations per week per user.
- **Transparency**: Users must see a "Why am I seeing this?" tooltip explaining the data insight.

## 2. Compliance
- Decisions must not use sensitive PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
- **Audit**: Monthly logs of all automated actions are reviewed by the Product Owner.
