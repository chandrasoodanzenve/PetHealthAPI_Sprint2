#  Growth Levers Intelligence & Prioritization Engine

## 1. Growth Levers

Key areas that drive business growth for PetHealth Pro:

- **Retention Lever**: Automated health alerts (Prevents Churn).
- **Engagement Lever**: Social features and pet achievement badges.
- **Revenue Lever**: Integrated vet consultation bookings.

## 2. Opportunity Scoring Methodology

We use the **RICE-R (Impact, Confidence, Effort, Risk)** scoring model:

- **Impact**: Expected increase in target metric (1-10).
- **Effort**: Engineering days required (Lower is better).
- **Confidence**: Data reliability (0.1 to 1.0).
- **Risk**: Operational or technical hurdles.

## 3. Short-term vs Long-term Trade-offs

- **Short-term**: Focus on high confidence, low effort tasks (e.g., Reminders).
- **Long-term**: Strategic bets with high impact but higher risk (e.g., AI-health prediction).
